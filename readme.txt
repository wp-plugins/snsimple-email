snSimple Email creates a simple email/contact form for your blog.

== Description ==

snSimple Email creates a simple email/contact form for your blog.  As well it gives you the option to display your email address and word verification.

= Features =

* The ability to show/not show your email address in the email form.
* If your email address is encoded in HTML entities to help prevent it from being harvested.
* Captcha word verification that can be turned on or off.
* jQuery form validation.
* Add the email form to a post or page by manually adding the shortcode or clicking on the insert simple form button in the visual editor.
* Add the email form to a template file with do_action.

== Installation ==

= 1. Upload to your plugins folder, usually `wp-content/plugins/` =

The plugin is in the form of a directory called 'sn-simple-email'.

= 2. Activate the plugin on the plugin screen. =

= 3. Change settings on the "Settings > snSimple Email" options screen. =
